Welcome

DPoS supernodes are part of the Elastos ecosystem security. They participate in validating ELA Transactions

Your Vote Matters

As a token holder, you can delegate your ELA to the supernodes securing the network, and earn the rewards generated

1 ELA is 1 Vote for 36 Nodes

When you vote, each ELA gives you 1 voting power. You don't spend your ELA. You can get your ELA back any time

ELA Transactions

If you make payment transactions using your ELA, your votes are cancelled and you need to vote again
